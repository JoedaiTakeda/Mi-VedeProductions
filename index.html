Biography

Anna Leigh Shaw

Anna Leigh Shaw is a producer, filmmaker, and storyteller with more than a decade of experience in film and television production. Throughout her career, she has guided projects from initial concept development through production, post-production, and distribution, combining creative vision with practical expertise at every stage of the filmmaking process.

As a producer, Anna has contributed to a growing slate of film and television projects, including the television series County Rescue (2024–Present) and the upcoming productions Between the Lines, Dysrhythmia, and A Secret Royal Romance. Her work is driven by a passion for meaningful storytelling, strong collaboration, and creating content that resonates with audiences.

Through Mi Vede, Anna continues to champion stories that reflect creativity, authenticity, and purpose while seeking to share God's love through the art of filmmaking.
